# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Sleepy History is built first for one history-curious iPhone owner who listens at night, often in bed, with low ambient light and low patience for fiddly controls. The user wants long, calm, original historical audio that can be generated, resumed, saved, and played with the phone locked.

## Product Purpose

The product creates original, historically grounded bedtime stories with narration and cover art. Success means the user can generate a story from a simple historical prompt, trust the source-backed result, and fall asleep without visual clutter, bright interaction, or playback surprises.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, polished, intelligent. Sleepy History should feel like a modern private audio library, closer to Apple Podcasts and Spotify in trust and restraint than to a fantasy notebook or demo app.

## Anti-references

Avoid ornate pseudo-historical decoration, generic AI gradients, nested glass cards, fake catalog filler, busy dashboards, marketing hero screens, social-feed patterns, and any UI that implies an affiliation with Apple Podcasts, Spotify, or Boring History For Sleep.

## Design Principles

1. Put listening first: resume, generate, and play should be reachable with minimal thought.
2. Keep the night mode honest: dark, legible, calm, and low glare without murky contrast.
3. Let real story assets carry the visual identity: cover art, transcript, sources, and progress should feel integrated rather than bolted on.
4. Prefer Apple-native familiarity: standard navigation, sheets, menus, lists, sliders, and clear hit targets.
5. Hide operational complexity until it matters: provider status, enrollment, retries, and storage controls should be available but not front-and-center.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Support Dynamic Type, VoiceOver labels for media controls and story actions, sufficient contrast in dark mode, large iPhone reachability, reduced-motion behavior, and readable controls while sleepy. Avoid tiny text inside essential controls and preserve lock-screen/background playback semantics.
